# Steam Civilization · 蒸汽文明

A cinematic bilingual long-scroll atlas of the hidden continuity between the
First Industrial Revolution and modern civilization.

> Humanity believes it entered the *electrical age*, then the *digital age*,
> then the *AI age*. But beneath every abstraction, modern civilization still
> runs on an upgraded form of steam.
>
> 人类以为自己依次进入了「电气时代」、「数字时代」、「AI 时代」。
> 但在所有抽象的下面，现代文明依然运行在一种被升级的蒸汽之上。

Six sections trace the thermodynamic core — `HEAT → PRESSURE → ROTATION →
ELECTRICITY → CIVILIZATION` — from Watt's beam engine in 1769 through the
Parsons turbine, nuclear reactors, hyperscale data centers, and the
planetary heat engine of the 21st century.

## Stack

Next.js 14 (App Router) · React 18 · TypeScript · Tailwind CSS ·
hand-built SVG diagrams (Watt engine cross-section, multi-source Sankey,
log-scale energy density ladder, rotating turbine, pressure gauges) ·
canvas-2D volumetric steam + ember field.

## Sections

1. **The Age of Steam · 蒸汽时代** — First Industrial Revolution, Watt's beam engine
2. **The Great Misunderstanding · 伟大的误解** — reciprocating vs. rotational; the 1884 Parsons hinge
3. **The Turbine Civilization · 涡轮文明** — ~85% of world electricity still comes from spinning a steam turbine
4. **Data Centers Are Modern Factories · 数据中心是新时代锅炉房** — AI infrastructure as 19th-century mill
5. **Thermodynamics Is the Real Emperor · 热力学才是真正的统治者** — energy density, entropy, civilization ceilings
6. **We Still Live Inside the Steam Age · 我们依然活在蒸汽时代** — the final reveal
